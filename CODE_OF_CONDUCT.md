# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project environment.

## Expected Behavior
1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.
3. Assume good intent and collaborate openly.

## Unacceptable Behavior
1. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
2. Personal attacks or intimidation.
3. Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this policy.
